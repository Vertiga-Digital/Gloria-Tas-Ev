About Us

Located in the heart of Avanos, Gloria Taş Ev is a stone-carved cave restaurant that reflects the region's unique historical texture. We bring local flavors and true Turkish hospitality to our guests in a warm, authentic, and cozy atmosphere.

Featured Flavors

Signature Dish

Pottery Kebab (Testi Kebabı)

The unique harmony of beef and vegetables slow-cooked for hours in clay pots made from Avanos earth. The traditional flavor of Cappadocia.

Wood-Fired Pita (Pide)

Fresh pita varieties prepared in our stone oven over a wood fire, with a crispy crust and abundant toppings.
